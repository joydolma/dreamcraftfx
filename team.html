About Our Company

Our Story

Dreamcraft FX Ltd was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate visual effects artists with a vision to revolutionize the industry. Over the years, we've grown from a small startup to a global leader in VFX, working on some of the biggest blockbusters and most popular TV shows.

Our commitment to innovation, quality, and creativity has earned us numerous awards and the trust of major studios worldwide. With offices in five cities across two countries, we continue to push the boundaries of what's possible in visual effects.
